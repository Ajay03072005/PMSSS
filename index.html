About PMSSS

The Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) was launched by the Government of India to provide financial assistance to students from Jammu and Kashmir pursuing higher education. The scheme supports students in professional and general courses by covering tuition fees and providing a maintenance allowance.
